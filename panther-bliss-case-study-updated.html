Panther Bliss (2024)

A multi-day community celebration featuring skate night and alumni basketball events that reached capacity attendance

Panther Bliss

Weekend of Community Events | 2024

Client

Murphy High School

Duration

4 months planning, 3-day event

Revenue Generated

$80,000

Attendance

3,000+ people across events

Project Overview

Panther Bliss was a comprehensive weekend of events organized in 2024 to celebrate the Murphy High School community while raising funds for athletic programs. As the Project Manager, I designed and executed a three-day series of themed events that culminated in record attendance and significant fundraising success.

The weekend series included multiple events targeting different segments of the community, with each event carefully choreographed to maximize attendance and engagement. The successful execution led to funding for new letterman jackets, uniforms for sports teams, and the establishment of a scholarship fund set to launch in late 2025.

Event Schedule

Thursday Evening

Skate Night

A community skating event that brought together students, families, and alumni for a night of fun and nostalgia. The event featured themed music, skating competitions, and exclusive merchandise sales.

Friday Evening

Alumni Basketball Game

A highlight of the weekend that reached maximum capacity attendance. Former Murphy High School basketball stars returned to compete in a thrilling exhibition game that packed the gymnasium to standing room only.

Weekend Activities

Community Celebration

Additional weekend activities included food vendors, community performances, sports clinics, and fundraising auctions to maximize community engagement and revenue generation.

Challenges

  • Coordinating a multi-day event schedule with different logistical requirements for each activity
  • Managing venue capacity constraints, particularly for the high-demand Alumni Basketball Game
  • Ensuring seamless transitions between events while maintaining security and safety protocols
  • Coordinating multiple stakeholders including corporate sponsors, school administration, alumni groups, and community partners
  • Creating unique marketing strategies for each event while maintaining consistent branding
  • Managing a complex budget with clear objectives for fundraising goals across multiple revenue streams

Approach & Strategy

I implemented a comprehensive project management approach using both traditional and agile methodologies to ensure successful event delivery:

  • Multi-Event Planning: Developed a cohesive master schedule with detailed sub-plans for each individual event
  • Capacity Management: Implemented ticketing and registration systems to manage attendance expectations and ensure venue safety
  • Stakeholder Engagement: Created a cross-functional committee with representatives from the school, corporate sponsors, and community leaders to align objectives and secure buy-in
  • Procurement Strategy: Developed strategic vendor partnerships to maximize value and minimize costs, applying my supply chain expertise
  • Data-Driven Planning: Utilized analytics from previous events to inform decisions on venue layout, event timing, and attendee flow
  • Risk Management: Created comprehensive contingency plans for weather, security, and other potential disruptions

The event's success led to significant community impact:

Project Management Approach

Managing a multi-day event series required sophisticated project management techniques:

  • Master Schedule Management: Created an integrated timeline with dependencies across all events
  • Resource Allocation: Strategically assigned personnel and equipment across events to maximize efficiency
  • Budget Management: Tracked all expenses against projected revenue to ensure profitability
  • Vendor Management: Coordinated multiple vendors including catering, entertainment, security, and technical services
  • Stakeholder Communication: Maintained regular updates with all stakeholders through structured reporting
  • Venue Logistics: Developed detailed plans for venue setup, breakdown, and transition between events
  • Attendee Experience Design: Created journey maps to ensure positive experiences at each touchpoint

Alumni Basketball Game Highlight

The Friday night Alumni Basketball Game emerged as the signature event of the weekend, reaching maximum capacity attendance. Special features included:

  • Return of notable Murphy High School basketball alumni spanning multiple decades
  • Special halftime performances and recognition ceremonies
  • VIP seating section for major donors and community leaders
  • Live streaming for community members unable to attend in person
  • Custom merchandise commemorating the event
